Animals

We love animals and have a variety for you to meet and feed while here. We want you to be able to come and see our animals and help us feed them and walk amongst them.

Currently we have many birds including tons of chickens, some guinea fowl, a duck, and hundreds of diffrernt varieties of birds that hang around.

The Goats often have kids around, walking slowly will help get you closer to them, our sheep can be a bit skittish but there is a few that will come and greet you. Our horses will likely stay away from you but sometimes come closer for an inspection if you have food or not.

Don't forget to spot a Kangaroo, echidna and Turtle or a sly fox late at night!

This video is about one of our poddy ( house ) lambs doing some karate kicks. The lamb is called Brock. He came to us when his mum rejected him only a few days old. Our son Jack took on the role of mum, fed him every 3 hrs including night time. Brock quickly became part of the family and antics a source of joy. Brock would prefer to be in one of our beds in our home but instead we found him another poddy lamb named Angel and they live with all the other sheep now. Our lambs have been a great education and experience for our kids who where living in Sydney up until just after the first Covid Lockdown.
